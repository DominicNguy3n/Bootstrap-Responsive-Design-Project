Top 5 agents in Valorant

Jett

Jett is an agile duelist known for her mobility and precision. She excels at fast-paced engagements and outmaneuvering opponents with her nimble abilities.

Abilities

  • Cloudburst: Throws a cloud of fog that obscures vision.
  • Updraft: Propels herself vertically, allowing her to reach elevated positions.
  • Tailwind: Dashes a short distance in the direction she's moving.
  • Blade Storm (Ultimate): Equips throwing knives that deal lethal damage, instantly killing opponents with a headshot.

Yoru

Yoru is a stealthy duelist who thrives on deception and misdirection, confusing enemies and controlling the flow of the game with his unpredictable movements. He excels at flanking and disrupting enemy strategies.

Abilities

  • Gatecrash: Places a rift tether at a targeted location. Activating the ability again teleports Yoru to the tether's location.
  • Blindside: Throws an unstable dimensional fragment that briefly flashes and deafens enemies upon detonation.
  • Fakeout: Deploys an echo that mimics Yoru's footsteps when activated, creating audio distractions for enemies.
  • Dimensional Drift (Ultimate): Enters a dimension rift, becoming invisible and invulnerable to damage. Yoru can freely move through the map for a short duration or cancel the ability to return to his original location.

Phoenix

Phoenix is a fiery duelist with a penchant for aggressive plays and self-sustainability. His abilities revolve around manipulating fire to control areas and overwhelm enemies.

Abilities

  • Blaze: Throws a wall of fire that blocks vision and damages enemies passing through it.
  • Curveball: Throws a flashbang that curves around corners, blinding opponents.
  • Hot Hands: Creates a healing zone upon impact, restoring Phoenix's health and damaging enemies.
  • Run it Back (Ultimate): Marks Phoenix's current location. If he dies during the ability's duration, or when the ability is deactivated, he respawns at the marked location.

Raze

Raze is an explosive duelist armed with powerful grenades and explosives, making her a formidable force on the battlefield. Her aggressive playstyle and ability to disrupt enemy positions make her a key asset for any team.

Abilities

  • Boom Bot: Deploys a small robot that moves forward, locking onto and chasing down the nearest enemy before exploding on contact.
  • Blast Pack: Equips a pack that sticks to surfaces and can be remotely detonated, launching Raze into the air and damaging enemies nearby.
  • Paint Shells: Throws a cluster grenade that explodes into smaller grenades, dealing damage to enemies caught in the blast radius.
  • Showstopper (Ultimate): Equips a powerful rocket launcher that fires a single explosive projectile, capable of dealing massive damage to enemies caught in its blast.

Reyna

Reyna is a relentless duelist who harnesses the power of her fallen foes to dominate the battlefield. With her vampiric abilities and aggressive playstyle, she excels at turning fights in her favor and single-handedly carrying her team to victory.

Abilities

  • Leer: Conjures an ethereal eye that travels in a straight line, blinding and impairing the vision of any enemies it passes through. Reyna can activate the eye to detonate it, temporarily blinding enemies caught within its radius.
  • Devour: Consumes the soul of a slain enemy, rapidly restoring her health over time. The ability allows Reyna to sustain herself during intense firefights and recover from damage taken.
  • Dismiss: Fades into the shadows, becoming invulnerable and gaining increased movement speed for a short duration. Reyna can use Dismiss to reposition herself quickly or escape dangerous situations.
  • Empress (Ultimate): Unleashes her full potential, entering a heightened state of frenzy that enhances her speed, fire rate, and abilities. While Empress is active, Reyna becomes a formidable force on the battlefield, dominating her enemies with unmatched aggression and precision.
